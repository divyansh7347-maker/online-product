Amazon was founded in the garage of Bezos' rented home in Bellevue. Bezos' parents invested almost $245,573 (equivalent to $506,800 in 2024) in the start-up. On July 16, 1995, Amazon opened as an online bookseller, selling the world's largest collection of books to anyone with World Wide Web access

The motive is to capitalize on the exponential growth of internet usage he saw in 1994, believing that the internet would revolutionize commerce and that selling books was an ideal starting point due to their universal demand and low cost
